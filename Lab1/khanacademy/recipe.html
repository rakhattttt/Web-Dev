Fluffy Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves:2-3 people
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1
Sugar 2 tbsp
Baking powder 1 tsp

Step 1: Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl.

Step 2: Add milk and egg, then whisk until smooth.

Step 3: Pour batter onto a hot pan and cook until golden.
